How to get to this strange country?

If you're too lazy to drive for 17 hours, don't worry - airplanes exist!

Both Vilnius and Kaunas are good options to fly to. But from the Netherlands there are two main options:

✈️ AirBaltic (Amsterdam -> Vilnius)
Comfortable, slightly more expensive.

✈️ WizzAir (Eindhoven -> Vilnius)
Can be cheaper, but is less reliable, and pay extra attention to the luggage options as it's included not in the price and can be expensive.
